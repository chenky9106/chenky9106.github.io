Publications

CogDPM: Diffusion Probabilistic Models via Cognitive Predictive Coding

K Chen, X Guo, Y Zhang, J Wang, M Long

International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML), 2024

This work introduces CogDPM, bridging diffusion models with cognitive science's Predictive Coding theory. We develop a novel precision estimation method using diffusion models' hierarchical sampling capabilities, enabling better attention allocation to signals with varying precision. The model demonstrates superior performance in predicting rigid body and fluid motion, as well as real-world weather forecasting tasks on U.K. precipitation and ERA surface wind datasets.

Skilful nowcasting of extreme precipitation with NowcastNet

Y Zhang, M Long, K Chen, L Xing, R Jin, MI Jordan, J Wang

Nature 619 (7970), 526-532, 2023

Citations: 210

NowcastNet is a groundbreaking model that combines physical-evolution schemes with conditional-learning methods for extreme precipitation forecasting. Evaluated by 62 professional meteorologists, NowcastNet ranked first in 71% of cases, particularly excelling in predicting extreme precipitation events with complex advective or convective processes.
